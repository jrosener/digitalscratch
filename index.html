Digital-Scratch

A vinyl disc emulation software. It's for DJs and it's free!

Learn more »

How does it work ?

Digital-Scratch is a software that analyzes motions (rotation speed and direction) of a vinyl disc played by a turntable.

It also provides a nice and simple player that play your favorite tracks allowing you to mix as you would do it with regular records.

Select a tune on the player and start your turntable. That's all, you are ready to mix!

Requirements

  • Up to 3 timecoded vinyl discs, see supported records
  • Up to 3 vinyl turntables
  • A GNU/Linux or Windows computer
  • A sound card driven by JACK providing a stereo input for each turntable
